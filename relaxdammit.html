--- layout: default title: Relax, DAMMIT --- # Relax, DAMMIT! album The concept for "Relax, dammit" had been evolving for years, but the phrase itself was born during a COVID-era call with a friend who owns a yoga studio. When I asked how she was managing financially, she admitted, "Not very good, to be honest. But I have enough for food, at least." Shocked, I asked, "Is it that bad?" "Yes," she replied.
